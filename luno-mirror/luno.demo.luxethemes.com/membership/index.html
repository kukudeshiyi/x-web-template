What's the difference between Free, Explorer, and Wanderer?

  • Free: Perfect for casual readers. Get our weekly newsletter, access to public articles, and community travel recommendations.
  • Explorer ($5/month): For travel enthusiasts who want more. Enjoy full access to destination guides, members-only tips, ad-free reading, and monthly curated itineraries.
  • Wanderer ($15/month): The ultimate experience. Get everything in Explorer plus early access to content, exclusive retreat guides, downloadable planning templates, and priority support.

Can I save with an annual subscription?

Yes! Annual subscriptions give you two months free. Explorer is $50/year (instead of $60) and Wanderer is $150/year (instead of $180). It's our way of thanking committed travelers.

How do I upgrade or change my plan?

Easy! Log into your account, go to "Your Account" section, and click "Change plan." Upgrades take effect immediately, and you'll only be charged the prorated difference. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. There are no contracts or commitments. Cancel anytime from your account settings, and you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. No questions asked.

How do I access members-only content?

Once you're signed in, members-only content is automatically unlocked based on your tier. Look for the "Members" or "Premium" labels on articles. You can also filter content to show only what's available to you.

Need help or have questions?

We're here for you! Reach out through our Contact page or email us directly. Free members typically hear back within 48 hours, while Explorer and Wanderer members enjoy priority support with responses within 24 hours.
