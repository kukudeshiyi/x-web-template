What's included with each theme purchase?

Every theme includes the complete source code, detailed documentation, free lifetime updates, and access to our support team. You'll also get demo content to help you get started quickly.

Do themes receive free updates?

Yes! All themes receive free updates for life, including new features, Ghost compatibility updates, bug fixes, and security patches. Once you purchase, you'll always have access to the latest version.

How do I install a Ghost theme?

Installing is simple: download the theme ZIP file, go to your Ghost admin panel, navigate to Settings → Design, and upload the ZIP. Your new theme will be ready to customize in seconds.

Can I use a theme on multiple sites?

Each license is valid for a single Ghost site. For multiple sites, you can purchase additional licenses at a discounted rate. Contact us for volume pricing.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide dedicated email support for all customers. Whether you need help with installation, customization, or have questions about features, our team typically responds within 24 hours.
